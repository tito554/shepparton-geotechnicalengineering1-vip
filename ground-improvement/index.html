In Shepparton, ground improvement addresses the challenges posed by the Goulburn Valley’s deep alluvial clays and silts, which are often prone to settlement and low bearing capacity. Our approach integrates local geotechnical conditions with Australian Standards (AS 3798, AS 2870) to deliver compliant, effective solutions. This begins with rigorous site characterisation, including unsaturated soil analysis to predict volume change behaviour, and extends to advanced grouting design for permeability reduction and strength gain in granular or fractured strata.

These techniques are critical for infrastructure, commercial buildings, and residential subdivisions where natural ground cannot support design loads. Managing problematic organics is equally vital; our organic soil management strategy ensures long-term stability for earthworks over peaty deposits. For settlement control without heavy surcharge, we also apply preloading design that leverages the site’s natural moisture regime. Every solution focuses on verifiable performance, reducing post-construction movement and project risk.
